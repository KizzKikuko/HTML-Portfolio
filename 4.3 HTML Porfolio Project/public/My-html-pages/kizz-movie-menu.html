My top 5 movies to watch on a rainy day

Very reminiscent of childhood, Studio Ghibli always has fantactic story telling and beautiful artwork.

Magical, great actors and story telling, fantastic to re-watch again and again.

I like the concept of the Kaiju and Jeagers, awesome visual effets and good balance of action to story.
